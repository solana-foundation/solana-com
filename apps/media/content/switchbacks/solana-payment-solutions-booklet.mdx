##### Your Playbook for Building on Solana's Payment Rails

Solana is high performance blockchain trusted by leading financial institutions at internet scale. Its modular ecosystem lets you compose exactly the stack you need. This booklet contains five use-case playbooks — Embedded Finance, Global Payouts, Merchant Experiences, Remittances, and Treasury Solutions — each mapping to a specific fintech vertical with partner recommendations, integration paths, and architecture guidance.

Trusted by Western Union, PayPal, Visa, Worldpay, and Fiserv.
