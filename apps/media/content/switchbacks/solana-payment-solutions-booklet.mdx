Pre-funding requirements, multi-day settlement windows, fragmented regional infrastructure — stablecoins on Solana eliminate all three. This booklet maps Solana's payments stack to five verticals:

- Embedded Finance
- Global Payouts
- Merchant Experiences
- Remittances
- Treasury Solutions

Each includes integration paths, architecture guidance, and a vendor landscape to help you identify the right partners for your stack.

Trusted by Western Union, PayPal, Visa, Worldpay, and Fiserv.
