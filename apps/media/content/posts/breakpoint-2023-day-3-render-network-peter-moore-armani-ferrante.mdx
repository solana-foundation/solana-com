It may have been a blustery day, but Breakpoint still shone on! Day 3 was full of ecosystem announcements, exciting talks, and historical retrospectives. Here’s the highlights.

### Session Highlights

- Video game veteran and former executive at Sega, XBox, and Electronic Arts [Peter Moore](https://youtu.be/eFN-IeVmZgg?feature=shared) gave a look back at the past — and future — of the video game industry.
- Veteran developer Armani Ferrante dug into some of [the future plans](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kSG-NK8qldc) of Backpack and X.
- At “Creating Opportunities for Artists and Collectors” on the Innovator Stage, the Solana Foundation announced it and the greater community will be heading to [Art Basel Miami](https://twitter.com/SolanaFndn/status/1720046867394810036) this December. ExchangeArt also dropped [previews](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=a_AG7teev7I) on some upcoming plans.
- On the Developer Stage, Steven Luscher of Solana Labs introduced the [new web3.js](https://youtu.be/JUJtAPhES5g?feature=shared).
- On the Main Stage, Trevor Harries-Jones [announced that Render Network](https://smb.greenvilleadvocate.com/article/Render-Network-Completes-Its-Successful-Upgrade-To-Solana?storyId=6543c85d2661f32216c74ff2), the world’s leading decentralized compute network, has successfully upgraded its core infrastructure from Ethereum to Solana.
- Star Atlas’ Michael Wagner took to the Main Stage to preview [what’s to come](https://twitter.com/staratlas/status/1720038598211162328) to the browser-based game.
- Hue Rhodes announced the [launch of FRIDAY](https://blockster.com/friday-protocol-unveils-mobile-app-at-solana-breakpoint-empowering-users-to-reclaim-their-data), a mobile consumer app that gives users access to their web2 data.
- See all Day 3 panels [here](https://www.youtube.com/@SolanaFndn).

### Event Highlights

- Aurory has been [previewing their blockchain game](https://twitter.com/SolanaConf/status/1720106154200572376?t=Yi90q-wo-kT5Ich_4_eTyQ&s=19) on the Innovator Stage!
- Also at the Innovator Stage, Raposa Coffee has been creating [PFP lattes](https://twitter.com/beeman_nl/status/1720094839357026528) at their booth!
