The Solana Foundation is committed to diversity, equity, inclusion, and respect at Foundation events. This Code of Conduct sets the minimum standard for participation and applies to all attendees, including Foundation employees and contractors, volunteers, speakers, vendors, and sponsors.

By participating in a Foundation event, attendees agree to follow the policies below. At the Foundation’s discretion, anyone deemed in violation may be removed from the event venue immediately without a refund and barred from future Foundation events.

## Expected behavior

The Foundation asks all participants to help maintain a safe, inclusive, and enjoyable environment.

- Be present and participate actively.
- Be mindful of your surroundings and of your fellow participants.
- Act with respect toward everyone you encounter.
- Be supportive, open, and willing to learn.

## Prohibited behavior

The following conduct violates this Code of Conduct.

- Inappropriate physical contact.
- Unwelcome sexual attention.
- Display of sexual images in public spaces.
- Deliberate verbal or physical intimidation.
- Sustained disruption of talks or other events.
- Advocating for or encouraging any of the above behaviors.

## Enforcement

This Code of Conduct embodies the Foundation’s core values and represents a minimum standard of conduct that the Foundation believes is necessary to ensure a safe, inclusive, and enjoyable environment.

The Foundation reserves the right to deny entry to, or eject from, Foundation events any person whose in-person or online conduct, either prior to, during, or subsequent to, a Foundation event, violates this Code of Conduct or its underlying principles in such a manner as to create a threat to the security or enjoyment of other attendees or future attendees.

Any person denied entry to, or ejected from, a Foundation event on these grounds shall not be entitled to a refund of any entry or event fee previously paid.

## Reporting an incident

If you experience or witness a violation of this Code of Conduct, please report it to [events@solana.org](mailto:solanaeventsteam@solana.org).

In case of a life-threatening situation or emergency, please call the national emergency number.

Thank you for your cooperation in making the Solana community a safer place for everyone and for your commitment to this Code of Conduct.
