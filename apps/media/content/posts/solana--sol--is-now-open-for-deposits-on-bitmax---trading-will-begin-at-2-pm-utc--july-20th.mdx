Pairs: SOL/USDTDeposits Open: 2 PM UTC on July 17th, 2020

Trading Starts: 2 PM UTC on July 20th, 2020

Total Prizes: ◎35,000

![](/uploads/posts/solana--sol--is-now-open-for-deposits-on-bitmax---trading-will-begin-at-2-pm-utc--july-20th/image-1.jpg)

The Solana Foundation is excited to share that SOL is now open for deposits on BitMax; starting at 2 PM UTC on July 20th, 2020, SOL/USDT will begin trading. Bitmax is a global digital assets trading platform based out of Singapore with over 190 trading pairs and 40 different margin trading pairs. BitMax is quickly becoming a leading exchange in China, Korea, and Japan.

[**Solana’s Official Educational Giveaway Campaign**](https://solana.com/earn/bitmax)**: ◎20,000 Available**

Event Duration: 07/17–07/23

The Solana Foundation is offering a SOL giveaway for BitMax users that learn more about Solana and join our global communities. To participate, BitMax users need to fill out a form, complete a quiz, follow the Solana [English Twitter](https://twitter.com/solana), join the Solana [English Telegram](https://t.me/solanaio) group, and refer users to the campaign.

Please review the following rules before registering.

- Duplicate submissions are not allowed. If your submission is found to be fraudulent or high-risk, you will be disqualified
- Delivery of the giveaway will happen within 96 hours after the campaign ends. \*Please keep an eye on the Solana Telegram and Twitter for a final 24-hour announcement
- The giveaway will be distributed to the Solana (SOL) address associated with the ID you provided upon registration
- The following countries are restricted from participating in this campaign: Burma, Crimea and Sevastopol, Cote d’Ivoire, Cuba, Democratic Republic of Congo, Iran, Iraq, Libya, North Korea, Sudan, Syria, and Zimbabwe
- BitMax will be distributing all token awards
- KYC is required and will be performed by BitMax

Here are the steps to participate:

1.  Complete the form at [https://solana.com/earn/BitMax](https://solana.com/earn/bitmax)
2.  Complete the short quiz
3.  Sign up for the Solana newsletter
4.  Follow [@solana](https://twitter.com/solana) on Twitter
5.  Join the Solana [Telegram](https://t.me/solanaio) group

This educational campaign allows users to be eligible for two sets of prizes: random drawings and referral competition wins. For users who place in the top 1,000 of participants, we will deliver a bonus giveaway on top of the random drawing that all users will receive.

**BitMax Campaigns**

To help promote the SOL listing, BitMax will be holding two separate events for the Solana community. The first event is a community giveaway, and the second event is a trading competition. ◎13,433 will be rewarded to the Solana and BitMax community between these two events.

**BitMax Community Giveaway Campaign: ◎4,478 Available**

Event Duration: 07/20–07/25

BitMax has teamed up with prominent crypto communities and influencers for this event. Once the event goes live, you will be asked to complete a series of tasks. The first 1,000 participants to complete the tasks will each get 4 SOL. All rewards will be sent by BitMax. Follow the [BitMax Twitter](https://twitter.com/BitMax_Official) account and join the [BitMax Telegram](https://t.me/BitMaxioEnglishOfficial) channel to learn more about this initiative.

**BitMax Trading Campaign:** **◎8,995 Available**

Event Duration: 07/23–07/28

BitMax will be holding a trading competition starting July 23rd. For the duration of the event, the top 20 users ranked by SOL trading volume on the SOL/USDT BitMax trading pair will be able to earn from a token pool of **◎**4,400. The token distribution will be in proportion of the individual trading volume by rank. In addition, an extra **◎**4,400 will be distributed to everyone else who buys and sells SOL during this time frame. The allocation will be in proportion to individual trading volume until all the **◎**4,400 has been distributed.
