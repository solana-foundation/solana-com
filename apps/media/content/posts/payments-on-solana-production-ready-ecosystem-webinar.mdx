Global payments are stuck in the past: slow, expensive, and limited by banking hours. But a shift is happening. Payments on Solana are enabling money to move at the speed of the internet: instant, global, and virtually free.

In our recent webinar, **"Payments on Solana: A Production Ready Ecosystem,"** Sheraz Shere (Head of Payments) and Ilan Gitter (Head of Ecosystem Engineering) outlined why financial giants like Visa, PayPal, and Worldpay are choosing Solana.

## Why Solana?

It comes down to production-ready scale. **Solana processes more transactions than all other blockchains combined.** With fees at a fraction of a cent and sub-second finality, it is the only chain capable of handling the volume required for global finance.

## The 5 Key Verticals

The session broke down exactly how this infrastructure is redefining five core areas:

1. **Treasury Management:** Bypassing T+2 settlement for 24/7 liquidity.
2. **Remittances:** Replacing costly correspondent banking with instant transfers.
3. **Neobanking:** Powering next-gen wallets with yield and debit cards.
4. **Payouts:** Instant global payments for gig workers and creators.
5. **Merchant Settlement:** Eliminating the 3-day wait for funds.
