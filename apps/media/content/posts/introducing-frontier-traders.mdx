The future of global trading is taking shape as onchain volume begins to surpass centralized exchanges, and decentralized markets now compete in depth and asset offerings long reserved for traditional financial markets.

Onchain is the new frontier.

Solana Foundation is introducing [**Frontier Traders**](https://www.frontiertraders.com/), an exclusive community and program for onchain traders at the forefront of emerging assets, markets, and technology.

Frontier Traders offers a VIP rebates program, trading campaigns, and global events for traders who move size across Solana’s ecosystem. Solana Foundation is the core contributor backing the infrastructure and relationships that make this possible, with more industry-leading contributors to join.

Membership is open to all. As a Frontier member, you get access to trading campaigns and infrastructure partner benefits. To qualify for VIP rebates, you must meet specific monthly volume thresholds.

[**Apply at frontiertraders.com**](https://frontiertraders.com)

## **Frontier Traders VIP Program**

This chain-level trading program gives qualified VIPs rebates across any venue, access to infrastructure built for size, and a voice in shaping Solana's market structure.

Traditional VIP programs are venue-level. The **Frontier Traders VIP Program** operates at the network level, tracking your trading activity across all Solana venues in aggregate.

To qualify, you must meet the following tier criteria:

## **FAQs**

**Which venues qualify for VIP Program volume / OI?**

At launch, the following venues are are tracked Jupiter, Phoenix, Raydium, Backpack (wallet), Orca, Byreal, Phantom, Fomo, Titan, Dflow, Pump. fun, Axiom, Ondo, xStocks, Jupiter, OKX DEX. 

**Can I qualify if I'm not currently trading on Solana?**

Yes. If you can demonstrate volume on other venues and are considering moving activity to Solana, we'll use that as the basis for qualification. [Apply to join ](https://www.frontiertraders.com/onboarding)Frontier Traders and note your trading volume activity and current trading venues.

**Will I receive support migrating my trading activity to Solana?**

Yes. Qualified VIP members receive both technical support and warm introductions to teams that can get you live on Solana.

**What is priority RPC access?**

We're launching our initial priority RPC program in partnership with Triton and Helius. As a VIP member, your professional or enterprise plan is covered by the Solana Foundation.
