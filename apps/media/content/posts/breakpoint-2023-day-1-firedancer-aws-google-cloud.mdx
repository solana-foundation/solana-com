What an incredible Day 1 at Breakpoint! The energy is electric, the ideas are flowing, and the Solana community is stronger than ever. Here’s a recap of today's standout moments.

### Session Highlights

- During Solana Labs CEO and Solana co-founder Anatoly Yakovenko's opening keynote address, Dan Albert, executive director at the Solana Foundation, revealed that the first version of Firedancer — the ground-up rebuild of a Solana client by Jump Crypto that [blew minds](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Dh6Yn2Odyr4) at Breakpoint 2022 — is now [live on testnet.](https://twitter.com/solana/status/1719310929593057720)
- From the developer stage, Solana nodes are now available for quick deployment on AWS with the help of infrastructure as code app in AWS Blockchain Node Runners repository. [Learn more here](https://twitter.com/solana/status/1719336012961153403).
- Sling [unveiled](https://twitter.com/SlingMoney/status/1719360288367980579) their peer to peer payments platform, running on USDC.
- Solana data is [now live](https://twitter.com/solana/status/1719381083718861276) on Google Cloud BigQuery, Google Cloud's serverless data warehouse and excels in machine learning, AI, and business intelligence.
- At the Innovator Stage, Solana Labs announced that GameShift, their web3 game API, was now [in an open beta](https://twitter.com/SolanaConf/status/1719384809795883351).
- [Breakpoint 2024](https://x.com/solana/status/1719298160420335780?s=20) is scheduled for Sept. 19-21, 2024 in Singapore!
- See all Day 1 panels [here](https://www.youtube.com/@SolanaFndn/playlists).
