**For the first time in World Series of Poker history, players can buy into tournaments directly with crypto on Solana with zero processing fees. Solana Foundation will also serve as the official Presenting Sponsor of the 2026 World Series of Poker and 2026 World Series of Poker Paradise.**

Poker is a game of skill and luck that, in many ways, mirrors the trader mentality: thinking in bets, managing your bankroll, acting on incomplete information, anchored by math. Beyond the overlapping characteristics between the crypto and poker communities, poker also represents a massive global audience: more people play poker every month than golf and tennis combined ([roughly 100 million monthly players](http://www.worldpokertour.com/About_us/FAQ.aspx)), and it's one of the fastest-growing games in the world.

Today, the [World Series of Poker](https://www.wsop.com/) (WSOP®) – the longest-running and most prestigious poker tournament series in the world — announced a first-of-its-kind collaboration with Solana Foundation to bring crypto directly into the global poker experience. Beginning at the 57th annual summer event at Paris Las Vegas and Horseshoe Las Vegas, players can complete tournament buy-ins using Solana, [powered by MoonPay Commerce](https://www.moonpay.com/business/commerce). It's the first time in WSOP history that players can buy into tournaments directly with SOL or stablecoins on Solana, with zero transaction fees.

The integration expands further at the 2026 WSOP Paradise in The Bahamas this December, where tournament winners can receive payouts in stablecoins on Solana, enabling near-instant access to winnings and reducing the friction traditionally associated with international tournament payouts.

As part of the collaboration, Solana Foundation will also serve as the official Presenting Sponsor of the 2026 World Series of Poker and 2026 World Series of Poker Paradise. As WSOP returns to ESPN – reaching more than 130 countries and 300 million homes via ESPN networks – Solana will be prominently featured throughout the series. 

Starting today, Solana will be streaming the WSOP on [X via @Solana](https://x.com/solana). Games are also streamed daily on the [WSOP YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/WSOP) and on [X via @WSOP](http://x.com/WSOP).

## Digital Money at the Heart of the Poker Economy

The poker community has long been at the forefront of crypto adoption, embracing technologies like Solana for their efficiency and transparency. As a high-performance network, Solana is celebrated for its industry-leading transaction speeds — capable of processing thousands of transactions per second — and ultra-low costs, with average fees remaining below $0.001.

The buy-in experience is designed to be direct: purchase your ticket and secure your spot at the table. No conversions, no extra steps, no friction between your wallet and your seat in the tournament. 

By enabling players to buy in with and receive winnings in stablecoins on Solana, these initiatives allow:

* Faster settlement and streamlined payment processing for eligible events
* Greater accessibility for international players traveling to WSOP events
* Near-instant global settlement, with reduced friction for users
* Expanded payment flexibility for players participating in marquee tournaments worldwide

Introducing Solana-powered buy-ins and pay-outs modernizes how money moves through the poker ecosystem and reduces friction for players around the world.
