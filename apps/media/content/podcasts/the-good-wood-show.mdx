The Good Wood Show is a weekly Solana ecosystem podcast from the Mindfolk community. Hosted by BAD-RELIGION and CT Twenty, the show features long-form conversations with builders, artists, and operators working across NFTs, DeFi, games, music, culture, validators, and consumer apps.

Each episode focuses on the people behind Solana projects and the practical work involved in building, operating, and sustaining communities onchain.
