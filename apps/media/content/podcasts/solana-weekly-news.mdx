Solana Weekly News is a weekly podcast from SolanaFloor covering the biggest stories, market moves, builders, apps, culture, and ecosystem updates across Solana. Each episode gives listeners a fast, high-signal breakdown of what matters across the Solana ecosystem, from DeFi and consumer apps to infrastructure, tokens, NFTs, and community trends.

The show is produced by SolanaFloor and hosted by Solana ecosystem contributors who follow the market, builders, and community closely.
