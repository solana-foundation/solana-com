## Project goal

Explore how tokenized real-world assets change requirements across the full
product stack.

## Checkpoints

- Separate the onchain model from the offchain business logic.
- List the compliance, metadata, or custody assumptions in the flow.
- Capture which pieces belong in the product layer versus the protocol layer.
