## Lesson goal

Bring the device online quickly so it can be managed remotely and prepared for
the Solana-connected lessons that follow.

## Checkpoints

- Configure the Pi without relying on a monitor and keyboard.
- Validate that the device joins your network successfully.
- Capture the connection details you will need later.
