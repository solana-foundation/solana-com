Trading and DeFi applications live or die on their view of onchain state: your
fills, your users' positions, the protocol events you react to. This page covers
what to track; general indexing methods and infrastructure live in
[Indexing](/docs/tools/indexing).

## What DeFi builders typically index

- **Your own transactions** — signatures, fills, and failures, keyed to the
  strategy or user that generated them
- **Program events** — swaps, deposits, liquidations emitted as logs by the
  venues you integrate
- **Account state** — pool reserves, orderbook slabs, positions — either polled
  or streamed
- **Failed transactions** — on Solana failures are cheap and common;
  distinguishing "lost the race" from "logic bug" requires indexing failures too
