Issuers need an offchain view of their token for reconciliation, reporting, and
compliance monitoring. This page covers what a tokenized-asset issuer should
track; the general-purpose indexing documentation (methods, providers, and
infrastructure choices) lives in [Indexing](/docs/tools/indexing).

## What to index for a tokenized asset

- **Supply events** — every mint and burn, reconciled against your reserve or
  transfer-agent records
- **Transfers** — sender, recipient, amount (or encrypted-amount event for
  [confidential transfers](/docs/tokenization/private-transfers))
- **Holder set** — all token accounts for your mint and their owners, for
  cap-table style reporting
- **Compliance events** — freeze/thaw, pause/resume, permanent-delegate actions,
  and Token ACL list changes
