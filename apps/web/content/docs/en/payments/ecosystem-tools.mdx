---
title: Ecosystem Tools
description: Third-party platforms and services for accepting crypto payments
---

The Solana ecosystem offers a variety of tools and platforms that simplify accepting stablecoin payments, integrating with ecommerce platforms, and handling fiat-to-crypto conversion. These tools abstract much of the complexity of building payment systems from scratch.

## Coinflow

[Coinflow](https://coinflow.cash/) provides a drop-in checkout widget that accepts credit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers with instant settlement in USDC. Their platform handles compliance, fraud detection, and optional chargeback protection.

**Key features:**

- Instant Solana USDC settlement to your wallet
- Built-in fraud detection and optional chargeback indemnification

[Coinflow Documentation](https://docs.coinflow.cash/)

## Crossmint

[Crossmint](https://crossmint.com/) offers embeddable checkout for NFTs, tokens, and physical goods with support for credit cards, digital wallets, and stablecoins. Their platform creates custodial wallets on-demand for users who don't have one.

**Key features:**

- Low fee stablecoin payments for humans and agents
- Guest checkout with automatic wallet creation
- Global coverage across 197 countries

[Crossmint Documentation](https://docs.crossmint.com/payments/introduction)

## Helio (MoonPay Commerce)

[Helio](https://hel.io/) powers Solana Pay's Shopify integration and provides checkout widgets for any website. Their platform supports deposits, payment intents, webhooks, and headless payment flows.

**Key features:**

- Embeddable checkout widget
- Shopify integration via Solana Pay
- Webhook notifications for payment events
- Multi-token support with automatic conversion

[Helio Documentation](https://docs.hel.io/)

## Sphere

[Sphere](https://spherepay.co/) provides a unified API for moving money across currencies, chains, and counterparties. Their platform handles fiat-to-crypto conversions, multi-chain transfers, and compliance (KYC/KYB, sanctions screening) out of the box.

**Key features:**

- Single API for on-ramps, off-ramps, and cross-chain transfers
- Solana USDC/USDT support
- Built-in compliance and fraud prevention
- Virtual accounts and white-label ramp widgets

[Sphere Documentation](https://docs.spherepay.co/)

## Solana Pay for Shopify

[Solana Pay](https://solanapay.com/) is an open-source payment protocol with an official Shopify integration. Merchants can accept USDC and other stablecoins directly, with instant settlement and no intermediary fees.

**Key features:**

- Native Shopify app integration
- Accept USDC, USDT, PYUSD, and other tokens
- Instant settlement to merchant wallet

[Solana Pay Shopify Documentation](https://commercedocs.solanapay.com/)

## Stripe Stablecoin Payments

[Stripe](https://stripe.com/) supports stablecoin payments through their existing Checkout, Elements, Payment Links, and Billing products. Payments settle in USD with support for USDC on multiple chains including Solana.

**Key features:**

- Solana USDC Payments Works with existing Stripe integration
- Supports recurring payments and subscriptions

[Stripe Stablecoin Documentation](https://docs.stripe.com/payments/stablecoin-payments)
